My trip to Hanoi

I went to Hanoi this summer with a friend of mine. We went with her parents. It was a fun experience. We slept in a fancy hotel with was of a golden colour. There was a swimming pool, a table tennis table, a pool and even tennis courts. We visited the city, went to a historical prison, and went to my cousin's ao dai shop.

My trip to Sapa

We also went to Sapa, a cold city in northern Vietnam. We trekked, and visited villages. We saw rice plantations and gave little gifts to children such as pens or pencils.
